# Spent or Sold - Style.md

## Overall Vibe
Chesney "No Shoes" outlaw holler—barefoot pirate anthem with rum-soaked swagger, '70s smooth jazz-rock glide crashin' into "No Shirt" carefree roar (Nassau nights, Florida moons, doubloons blown on livin'). 70% sunscreen-soaked fun (tavern stomps, Havana grins), 30% gritty confession (empty pockets, no regrets). Think sly grooves meets "Beer in Mexico" proud—108 BPM swagger to 118 crescendo, for pier bonfires or Ron Jon tailgates, flip-flops kicked off in the foam.

## Key & Mode
- **Key:** E Aeolian (E F# G A B C D E)  
- **Why Aeolian?** Raw natural-minor bite—no sweet notes, just outlaw ache turnin' to triumph. Easy beginner: Open Em base, no tweaks—barres optional, hammock comfy.

## Tempo & Structure
- **Tempo:** Intro 100 BPM (tavern slow-burn), swell to 108 (swagger roll); crescendo final chorus 118 drum surge.  
- **Structure:** Intro → V1 → Chorus → V2 → Chorus → Bridge → Final Chorus (explode) → Outro.  
- **Length:** ~3:45—snappy rum swig, room for "hell yeah" hollers.

## Instrumentation
- Acoustic steel-string (fingerpick swagger, raw wood drive, easy Em-D-C shapes) + pedal steel (weeping bends, yacht-smooth twang)—standup bass (lazy walk to heavy thump), drums (suitcase stomp bass drum kick, conga/snare/tom tavern pulse)—raw intimacy, no overstack.  
- **Core:** Acoustic/steel duet leads the roar, bass anchors the sway, drums build to tavern thunder.  
- **Ad-Libs:** Coin clink percussive, tavern cheer stomp.

## Vocals & Harmonies
- **Lead:** Jax rasp—simmer verses (hooch to rum glow), Chesney holler choruses (fist-pump fire, no-shoes grin).  
- **Harmonies:** Light 1-2 part whispers on chorus (no gospel)—falsetto lift "spent or sold" (pirate pride), faint alto echo "hell yeah" sway; crescendo-full final roar. Solo rasp dominant, like bonfire boast.  
- **Delivery:** Sensory flow—sunscreen sting, tavern stomp barefoot, rum-caramel burn, doubloon clink. Punch: "hell yeah," "arrr" ad-lib, sand toes.

## Production Notes
- **Intro/Outro:** Acoustic
